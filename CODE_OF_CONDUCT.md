# Code of Conduct

This project aims to keep collaboration respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

## Expectations

- Be respectful and patient
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Keep discussions focused on the work

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discrimination of any kind
- Deliberate intimidation
- Bad-faith or abusive communication

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions and contributions to keep the space safe and productive.
